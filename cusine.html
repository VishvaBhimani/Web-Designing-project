North Indian Cuisine

North India is known for its rich, artomatic, and Flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani : A Fragent rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat and vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken : A Creamy, tomato based curry that's favourite worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka : Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

South Indian offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavors:

  • Masala Dosa : A Crispy rice creped stuffed with spices potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambhar : Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetables stew.
  • Chettinad Chicken : A fiery, Flavorful Chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.

South indian Favourite : Masla Dosa

Sweet Delights

No Indian meal is complete without including in some mouth-watering dasserts.

  • Gulab Jamun : Fired Milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla : Soft and Spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasam : a south Indian dessert made of milk, rice and jaggery.
